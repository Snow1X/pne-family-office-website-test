Our Approach

Since the P&E family’s founding, we’ve stayed sharply focused on long-term value investments. Recognizing such opportunities are rare, we act with precise timing, strong partnerships, targeted asset selection, and ample capital. Years of investing in partnerships, operations, networks, and portfolios have paid off—granting us frequent access to superior opportunities and steady wealth growth.

We Employ a Rigorous and Dynamic Investment Strategy for Every Investment Decision

Discern Trends and Clarify Issues

Our seasoned team identifies investment themes and long-term trends through market insights. We explore catalysts for each theme, closely track the evolution of investment logic, and lay a solid foundation for decision-making.

Plan, Act, and Deliver Results

We determine the best way to approach specific investment themes—via direct operation, direct investment, or fund managers. We invest decisively in our strengths, and partner with experts in other areas to leverage their advantages.

Risk-Controlled Portfolio

We select uncorrelated themes, pinpoint key market risks, and focus on fundamentals to control risks. Instead of following index weights, we prioritize risk-return trade-offs, building a stable investment portfolio with profit potential.
