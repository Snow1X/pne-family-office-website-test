About P&E Family Office

Founding Background and Mission

Founded in 2003 by Peter and his family, our original intention was to develop and expand family assets into a diversified group. Our core goal is to accumulate intergenerational wealth, provide long-term support for future generations, and achieve sustainable cross-generational growth of wealth.

Industry Position and Business Scope

Today, we have become one of Australia’s mature and successful family offices, with our business covering diverse asset classes such as real estate, energy transition, technological innovation, and cultural tourism & consumption. We not only handle wealth management for the family internally but also offer sustainable wealth creation opportunities to external investors.

Team Culture and Core Competencies

We are based on the spirit of cooperation and entrepreneurial culture. The team works closely together, focusing on screening high-quality investment opportunities. Through cross-field resource integration and long-termism strategy, we build a wealth growth path for multiple generations.
