About Us

P&E Family Office, a private family office specializing in intergenerational wealth creation, meticulously manages its diverse portfolio of investment funds. We collaborate with seasoned fund companies holding Australian Financial Services Licenses, anchoring our approach in a legacy-preserving perspective. Leveraging multi-dimensional thematic investment strategies as our edge, we precisely navigate and capitalize on market opportunities.

Our Journey

(2003-2014)
Inception and Business Expansion

Established in 2003 by Peter and his family, P&E Family Trust marked a pivotal milestone that kicked off the family’s journey of growth. As operations advanced, opportunities abounded. Over the subsequent two decades, the company expanded steadily into diverse industries and forged partnerships with major monopolistic enterprises.
-In 2003, the company pioneered entry into the telecommunications sector, initiating a diversified business layout.
-In 2005, P&E Family Trust shifted its strategy, refocusing from telecommunications to financial domains such as structured investments.
-In 2014, the family enterprise completed shareholding system reform and went public, secured a monopolistic operating license, and further expanded its overseas telecommunications business to include cross-border submarine cable operations.

Breakthroughs in the Telecommunications Sector

In the telecommunications sector, Peter and his family enterprise, leveraging innovative operational models and cutting-edge technologies, broke the industry's monopolistic landscape and established a significant position in China's telecommunications market. Their distinctive profit model, with telecommunications operations and structured investments as core businesses, laid a solid foundation for success.

Strategic Transformation and a New Chapter

In 2014, alongside the listing of Changxin Technology, P&E Family Office executed a strategic realignment: transitioning from a frontline operator to a behind-the-scenes steward, divesting direct control over the company, and relocating to Australia—thus ushering in a brand-new chapter in the development of the family enterprise.
