The Continuity of Family Legacy and Philanthropy

Peter and Eva aim to leave descendants a rich legacy—material wealth, but more so spiritual and cultural inheritance. Rooted in family involvement and intergenerational giving, their vision drives sustained philanthropy and social change, emphasizing Buddhist culture. They believe a charitable tradition teaches younger generations generosity’s value and their potential to drive change.

To realize this vision, via P&E Family Office and their family foundation, they support charitable and cultural initiatives—offering financial aid and opportunities for family members to engage firsthand, deepening understanding of Buddhist core values and philanthropy’s meaning. These efforts foster younger generations’ social responsibility and active involvement, planting seeds of compassion and wisdom.

Through long-term dedication, Peter and Eva have amassed a significant collection of cultural heritage, particularly religious statues. These holdings not only attest to their passion for cultural heritage but also serve as vital channels for cultural inheritance and exchange. By showcasing these treasures through public sharing sessions, they foster cultural understanding and preservation awareness.

Peter and Eva’s efforts set an example—for their family and society—of how individual and familial endeavors can advance social justice and spiritual-cultural development. Their life and educational philosophies reflect a profound cultural responsibility and far-sighted consideration for future generations, ensuring their spiritual legacy and cultural wealth endure and thrive.

Peter's Philosophy of Giving

01
Suffering hones, gratitude grounds

Peter’s giving philosophy grew from hardships in a unique historical period. After the Cultural Revolution, his family lost everything, and he started from scratch. Those struggles taught him: no career thrives alone—others’ support matters. "A drop of kindness, a flood in return" became his guiding light; this gratitude and giving form the foundation of his philosophy.

02
Entrepreneuring insights, giving back to society

As founder of P&E Family Office, Peter knows past glory yields to present effort. Entrepreneurial hardships fueled his passion to help. He believes rising through challenges demands giving back. Supporting others to build a supportive ecosystem, he sees aiding growth as self-blessing—gaining luck and fulfillment. This elevates his giving philosophy in practice.

03
Embodying in action, passing on culture

At P&E Family Office, Peter embeds his giving philosophy in operations and philanthropy—via the family foundation, strategic investments, employee donations and social initiatives—to support communities and startups. It shapes the firm’s culture, inspires all, and gains value through inheritance.

Art, Religion, Culture: The Mission and Vision of the Buddhist Association of Victoria

Driven by a deep commitment to cultural heritage and exchange, P&E Family Office has established the Buddhist Association of Victoria—no mere title, but a fruit of collective endeavor. It aims to foster mutual understanding and respect, showcasing humanity’s shared values and beauty through artistic and cultural diversity. With P&E’s leadership and the selfless support of community members and like-minded partners, the Association has launched with initial operational funds, heralding a new cultural exchange platform.

The Association upholds the principle of safeguarding and respecting everyone’s beliefs and values, striving to be a neutral platform. It promotes and celebrates the rich diversity of Chinese culture through educational and cultural activities such as art exhibitions, cultural seminars, and public lectures. Recognizing the critical need to avoid political and religious sensitivities in a pluralistic society, all its activities and initiatives focus on fostering cross-cultural understanding and harmony rather than sowing division.

Among its key initiatives, the "Southern Sky Buddha Day" project is the Association’s centerpiece. It will build the world’s tallest Sakyamuni Buddha statue in Tasmania, Australia—198 meters tall with a 58-meter pedestal. Upon completion, this landmark will attract global Buddhists and cultural enthusiasts. Supporting it will be Buddhist cultural events: grand ceremonies, academic forums, meditation workshops—all to spread Buddhist essence and boost cross-cultural understanding.

P&E Family Office and all supporters envision the Buddhist Association of Victoria as a bridge connecting people of diverse cultural backgrounds, fostering collective growth through understanding and respect. We firmly believe in the power of culture and art to transcend boundaries and unite hearts—and the Association, along with initiatives like the "Southern Sky Buddha Day" project, will be pivotal in driving this progress. Looking ahead, we aim for this platform to evolve into a vital force for promoting social harmony and appreciation of cultural diversity.

Medical Heritage Preservation: The Mission and Pursuit of P&E Family Office

Cherishing and Safeguarding Ancient Medical Wisdom

Deeply reverent of Chinese culture, P&E Family Office treasures time-honored medical wisdom like ancient TCM prescriptions and acupuncture—millennia-old gems vital to human health. It actively seeks, collects and preserves these legacies to pass on 5,000 years of wisdom and benefit the health of family members and friends.

Driving the Modern Application of Traditional Medicine

Our goal goes beyond preserving ancient medical knowledge and skills—we are committed to understanding, researching, and promoting their modern application. By collaborating with medical experts, scholars, and traditional medicine enthusiasts, we delve into the scientific principles behind these age-old therapies, exploring ways to apply them more broadly in contemporary health and treatment practices, thus revitalizing traditional medicine.

Spreading Health Concepts, Co-building a Brilliant Vision

P&E Family Office believes preserving medical heritage not only strengthens health safeguards for family and friends but also advances natural, harmonious lifestyles globally. Upholding tradition and nature as core values—our vision for future wellness—we seek global partners to protect and promote this legacy, extending the wisdom of ancient Chinese remedies and practices to more people.

Children's Education: Educational Initiatives of P&E Family Office

Invest in the Construction of Kndergartens

P&E Family Office's investment in Australia's education sector reflects its care for future generations and profound understanding of social responsibility and cultural integration. It has invested in building and operating two kindergartens in Victoria and Queensland, providing high-quality early education services for over 300 children. This is not just about offering educational services, but creating a loving and caring "second home" for the children.

Australia, a young and vibrant nation, sees education as a pivotal force in shaping its future. Though its history as a nation is relatively short, there is ample room for exploration and innovation in education. Leveraging its strong family network and capabilities, P&E Family Office actively contributes to Australia’s educational development, driving progress and innovation in the sector.

Enhancing International Exchanges and Cooperation

01  Building an educational cooperation network

Leveraging the family office’s global network, partnerships are forged with educational institutions worldwide. Advanced educational concepts and practices are introduced to broaden Australian children’s learning horizons and enrich educational resources.

02  Investing in educational technology innovation

In line with the trend of technological development, we invest in innovative enterprises in the edtech sector. We support the development of more efficient teaching tools and platforms, empowering education with technology to promote educational equity and accessibility.

03  Supporting teacher training and development

Quality teachers are central to enhancing educational quality. We fund teacher training programs to support their professional development and skill improvement, attracting more outstanding talents to the education sector.

04  Advocating a culture of lifelong learning

Through public lectures, seminars, community activities and other forms, we encourage people of all ages to keep learning and growing. This fosters a societal respect and passion for knowledge and education.
